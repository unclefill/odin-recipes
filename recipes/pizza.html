Mushroom & Herb Pizza

Mushroom and herb pizza, delicious!

This pizza is sure to be the star dish at any pizza party. What makes this pizza so special is the blend of fontina and mozzerella cheese. There is no sauce on this pizza, but don't let that fool you, it packs a flavor punch! Rosemary and thyme give this pie a special flavor when paired with the earthy mushroom tones. To top it all off, a drizzling of calbrian chili oil as well as honey and finally, the zest of one lemon. A true show stopper.
